About SkillSwap Hub

SkillSwap Hub is a community driven platform where learners, hobbyists, and creators can exchange skills, share learning resources, and support each other's growth.

Our Mission

To empower individuals of all backgrounds to learn, teach, and grow through a collaborative skills sharing community. Learning will be accessible, supportive, and fun and SkillSwap Hub will make that possible.

What You Can Do

  • Share your own learning resources
  • Browse community posts by category
  • Search, filter, and sort resources
  • Upload images with your posts
  • Comment on posts and join discussions
  • Create and update your personal profile
  • Request new skills from the community

Technology Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • LocalStorage for saving posts, profiles, and comments
  • Client-side dynamic rendering (no server needed)
  • Responsive layout for mobiles, tablets, and desktops
